Privacy Policy for AgainstALL

Effective Date: June 29, 2025

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application "AgainstALL" ("the Application") for mobile devices that was created by Agle.

Information We Collect

Our primary goal is to provide you with a stable and enjoyable gaming experience. Our backend service, Microsoft PlayFab, automatically creates a player account to store your game progress. This account is associated with your device and its IP address.

Non-Personal Information (Automatically Collected)

  • Device & Usage Data: The type of mobile device you use, your mobile device's unique device ID (like the Google Advertising ID), the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, and information about the way you use the Application.
  • Gameplay Data: Game progress, scores, virtual currency, earned skins, achievements, and other gameplay-related statistics are stored in your PlayFab player account.
  • Crash Reports: We collect crash data to identify and fix bugs.

How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide and Improve Our Game: To operate, maintain, and enhance the features of AgainstALL.
  • To Power Online Features: To manage your game account, including leaderboards, virtual currency, skins, and saved game data via PlayFab.
  • To Show Ads: To display third-party advertisements within the game.

Third-Party Services

Our game integrates third-party services. These third parties have their own privacy policies.

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Microsoft PlayFab

Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data. For detailed instructions on how to do this, please visit our Data Deletion page.

Children's Privacy

We do not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with information without their consent, they should contact us.
