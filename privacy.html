Privacy Policy for AgainstALL

Effective Date: June 29, 2025

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application "AgainstALL" ("the Application") for mobile devices that was created by Agle.

Information We Collect

Our primary goal is to provide you with a stable and enjoyable gaming experience. We collect information to improve our services, but we strive to collect only what is necessary.

Non-Personal Information (Automatically Collected)

  • Device & Usage Data: The type of mobile device you use, your mobile device's unique device ID (like the Google Advertising ID), the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, and information about the way you use the Application (e.g., session length, levels completed, in-game events).
  • Gameplay Data: Game progress, scores, achievements, and other gameplay-related statistics.
  • Crash Reports: We collect crash data to identify and fix bugs, ensuring a more stable game.

Personal Information

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name or phone number directly. If you contact us for support, we will keep a record of that communication, including your email address, to resolve your issue.

How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide and Improve Our Game: To operate, maintain, and enhance the features and functionality of AgainstALL.
  • For Analytics: To understand how players interact with our game, helping us improve user experience and game design.
  • To Show Ads: To display third-party advertisements within the game. These ads help us keep the game free to play. The information collected may be used to show you ads that are more relevant to your interests.
  • To Power Online Features: To manage online game features like leaderboards, virtual currency, skins, and saved game data.

Third-Party Services

Our game integrates third-party services for analytics, advertising, and backend functionality. These third parties have their own privacy policies. Please review them.

We use the following services:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Microsoft PlayFab
  • Unity Ads & Analytics

Your Rights & Choices (Opt-Out)

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling it using the standard uninstall process for your device. You can also opt-out of interest-based advertising on mobile applications by checking the privacy settings of your Android device.

Data Security

We are concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. We use administrative and electronic measures to protect information we process. However, no security system can prevent all potential security breaches.

Children's Privacy

We do not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with information without their consent, they should contact us. We will delete such information from our files within a reasonable time.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy here. Continued use is deemed approval of all changes.
